Coastal Cleanup

Wave goodbye to waste

Who are we?

We are a non profit organization that strives to keep the planet the way that we found it. We do this by hosting weekly coastal cleanups, picking up debris and waste that either gets left behind by tourists or washes onto shore. By doing this, we reduce litter and protect our oceans.

Our Mission

Our planet is made of 97% water, yet 40% is severly polluted. We are a nonprofit that is looking to expand globally to help lower the water pollution crisis.
